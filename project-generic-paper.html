A product showcase website

Generic Paper is a product showcase website addressing one of the most odinary objects: paper. What gives this project a great potential is that rarely the competitors focus on branding messages and the usability of their website where Generic Paper stands out.

BRANDING

The term “generic paper” is a paradoxic name. When a brand is created, the most “generic” quality is to be “unique”. But a piece of paper’s mission is to be generic, to not stand out because it needs to carry content and meanings. Essentially, to be “generic” is the driven force which influence people’s perception and choice.

The brand “generic paper” needs to connect to people in a way that they can easily pull over a piece of paper and do stuff with it, whether is is writing, drawing, folding or making. It is the tangibility and freedom makes people like paper.

A nice and clean piece of paper means a fresh start of something. There is nothing more inspiring then letting the ink run, and giving an idea a chance. The absence of established content marks the most prominent identity of “generic paper”.

Something is not “missing”. Something is about to be born.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

Most of the competitors focus on professional customers and ignores the consumer market. Rarely they demonstrate effort on strong and clear branding and their usability tend to be subpar. To make it worse, most of compatitors have outdated aesthetics which does envoke customer confidence.

User Flow

In order to achieve great usability, being able to find products from a broad catagory as quick as possible is critical. Therefore the best-selling product is listed as immediate access.

Site Map

Aside from ease of use, the home page has to have an interesting layout to attract visitor attention while remain its efficiency.
